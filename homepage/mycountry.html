My Country

Viet Nam

Vietnam is a Southeast Asian country known for its rich history. It is bordered by China to the north, Laos to the northwest, Cambodia to the southwest, and the South China Sea to the east. The country covers an area of approximately 331,210 square kilometers and has a population of around 97 million people, making it the 15th most populous country in the world.


The Vietnamese language, written in the Latin script (Quốc Ngữ), is the official language, and the đồng (VND) is the national currency.


The country's natural beauty attracts tourists from around the world, with highlights including the UNESCO World Heritage Sites of Ha Long Bay, Hoi An Ancient Town, and the Imperial City of Hue. Other popular destinations include the bustling cities of Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City (formerly Saigon), the serene Mekong Delta region, the picturesque rice terraces of Sapa, and the beautiful beaches along the coast like Da Nang, Nha Trang, and Phu Quoc Island.


Vietnamese cuisine is another major draw for visitors, as it features a unique combination of flavors, textures, and aromas that balance sweet, sour, salty, and spicy elements. Dishes like pho (noodle soup), banh mi (baguette sandwich), and bun cha (grilled pork and rice noodles) are popular both domestically and internationally.
